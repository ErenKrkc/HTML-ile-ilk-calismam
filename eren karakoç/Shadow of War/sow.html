  • Middle-earth: Shadow of War-TRAILER
    •

  • Middle-earth: Shadow of War is an action role-playing game developed by Monolith Productions and published by Warner Bros.

  • It is the sequel to 2014's Middle-earth: Shadow of Mordor, and was released worldwide for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One on October 10, 2017.

  • Middle-earth: Shadow of War is an action role-playing game set in an open world environment and played from a third-person perspective, following from its predecessor.

  • The player uses their combined abilities to complete various missions, typically aimed to disrupt the armies of Sauron.

  • The game includes main quests that drive the game's narrative, and numerous optional quests that can help the player boost Talion's abilities and Followers via the game's "Nemesis System".

  • In contrast to the previous game, which was more of a hack and slash, the sequel will have an action role-playing approach, creating a more personalized experience for each player.

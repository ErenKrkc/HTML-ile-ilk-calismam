  • F1 2017-TRAILER
    •

  • F1 2017 is a racing video game developed and published by Codemasters.

  • It was released for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, macOS and Windows on 25 August 2017.

  • The game is based on the 2017 Formula One season and includes all of the twenty circuits, twenty drivers and ten teams competing in the season.

  • F1 2017 is the ninth installment in the Formula One video games franchise developed by Codemasters.

  • The macOS version, developed by Feral Interactive, was released simultaneously with the other versions - a first in the series' history.

  • The game features an expanded team management mode, which offers players more control over research and development of car parts.

  • Engine components and gearboxes are subject to wear and will ultimately fail, with players receiving grid penalties for exceeding their quota of components.
